Animation Beach Theme

This Java-based project features a beach-themed animation that transitions between a lively daytime scene and a tranquil night, showcasing dynamic environmental changes. During the day, boats sail across the sea, cars move along the road, and trees sway by the promenade. As the scene shifts to night, the sky darkens, revealing snow and the moon, creating a peaceful atmosphere. The animation highlights the contrast between day and night, demonstrating Java’s ability to handle smooth transitions and detailed graphical rendering, offering an immersive experience of time-driven changes in the environment.
